MCCCS Towhee: Ding-Andersen 1986

 

 

 Overview
    This section covers the Ding-Andersen germanium parameters as they are implemented into the towhee_ff_Ding1986 file in the ForceFields directory. All of the Towhee atom types for this force field are listed, along with a short description of their meanings. This force field was intended for solid germanium. It uses the Stillinger-Weber potential type. Any discrepencies (especially typos) from the published values are the sole responsibility of Marcus Martin, and I welcome feedback on how this implementation compares with other programs.
