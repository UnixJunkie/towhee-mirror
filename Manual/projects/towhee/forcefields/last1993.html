References for Lastoskie et al. 1993
    This is not a comprehensive force field. It is simply the Lennard-Jones parameters used to represent the dinitrogen (N2) molecule in a Carbon Slit Pore.

Lastoskie et al. 1993 in Towhee
    The official force field name for Lennard-Jonesium in Towhee is 'Last1993'. This list contains the atom names in the towhee_ff_Last1993 file, along with a brief description. Please note that the capitalization and spacing pattern is important and must be followed exactly as listed here.
